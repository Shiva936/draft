# Code Of Conduct

Draft is an open-source project for local-first change review. Project spaces should stay practical, respectful, and focused on making the software safer and clearer.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of different experience levels and working styles.
- Keep technical criticism specific, actionable, and about the work.
- Assume public project discussions may be read by new users and contributors.
- Report security issues through [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md), not public threads.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Pressuring maintainers to bypass safety, review, or disclosure processes.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may moderate issues, discussions, pull requests, and other project spaces to keep collaboration productive. Enforcement can include warnings, edited or hidden comments, temporary restrictions, or removal from project spaces.

## Reporting

For conduct concerns, contact the maintainers through the project’s listed community or maintainer channels. If the concern is security-sensitive, use [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
